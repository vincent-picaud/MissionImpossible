* Examples
** Hessian action $H.v$

Observe that
$$
H.v=\nabla_X \langle v,\nabla_X f \rangle
$$

Check:

$$
\nabla_X v . \nabla_X f = \nabla_X  \langle v, \sum_j \partial_j f \rangle =\sum_{ij} v_i \partial_{ij}f = H.v
$$
